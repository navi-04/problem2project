Frequently Asked Questions

How can I contribute problems to the platform?

Currently, we're working on community submission features. For now, please reach out via email with your problem statements, and our team will review them for inclusion.

Is the platform free to use?

Yes! Problem2Project is completely free to use and open-source. We believe in democratizing access to innovation opportunities.
